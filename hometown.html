The Equator Monument, marking the position where Pontianak is right on the equator.

Pontianak, my hometown, is the capital city of West Borneo. It is a small, comfy town where there is everything you can find in a big modern city. It is also home to the fifth-longest river in East Asia, the Kapuas River. Being on the equator, it is also known for its warm tropical climate.
