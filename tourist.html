Tourist Places

Masjid Jami'

The Jami' Mosque is a mosque near the Sultan building. With a scenery just beside the Kapuas river and a traditional melayu designed mosque, it truely has its own unique feel to pray in the Jami' Mosque.

Keraton Kadariah

Keraton Kadariah is a palace of the Kadariah sultan dinasty. Though pontianak does not maintain a kingdom system for democracy anymore, the Kadariahs still have a big influence in the society.
